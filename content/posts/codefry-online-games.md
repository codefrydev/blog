**Free browser games with friends — no download, no account, no hassle.**

## What is CodeFry Online Games?

**CodeFry Online Games** is a free collection of classic and strategy games you can play right in your browser. Open the site, pick a game, and you're in — on desktop, tablet, or phone.

Whether you want a quick solo match, a game on the same screen with someone beside you, or a full online session with friends across town or across the world, it's built for all of that in one place.

**Live at:** [onlinegames.codefrydev.in](https://onlinegames.codefrydev.in)

## Why we built it

Game night shouldn't mean everyone installs the same app, creating accounts, or waiting on updates. We wanted something simpler:

- **Instant access** — open a link and play
- **Real multiplayer** — not just hot-seat on one device, but proper online rooms with friends
- **A growing library** — one home for chess, party games, and board classics
- **Completely free** — no subscriptions, purchases, or hidden fees

CodeFry Online Games is that hub: familiar games, modern convenience, zero friction.

## Games you can play today

The catalog is growing. Right now you'll find:

### ♟️ Chess

Classic two-player chess. Play locally on one device, challenge the AI, or host an online room and invite a friend. Strategy, tension, and timeless rules — anywhere you have a browser.

### ⚛️ Chain Reaction

A grid-based strategy game where you place atoms and trigger cascading explosions. Outlast your opponents until only one player remains. Supports **2 to 8 players**, so it works for duels or a full group session.

### 🎲 Ludo

The board race everyone knows: roll the dice, move your tokens, and be the first to get all four home. Play locally or online with **2 to 4 players**, including AI opponents when you need them.

More titles are on the way. The home page always shows the full, up-to-date list.

## How online play works (the simple version)

1. **Pick a game** from the catalog.
2. **Choose online multiplayer** and create a room.
3. **Share the room code** with your friends.
4. They open the same game in their browser, enter the code, and join.
5. **Play together** in real time — with optional chat and video so it feels like you're at the same table.

No app store. No sign-up wall. Just a link, a code, and your friends.

## Play your way

Every game supports different modes depending on what you need:

| Mode | Best for |
|------|----------|
| **Local play** | Two or more people on the same device — great for travel or the couch |
| **Online multiplayer** | Friends in different places, connected through a shared room |
| **AI opponents** | Practice or play when you don't have someone nearby (where the game supports it) |

You can set a display name when joining online rooms. A full account isn't required to browse or play.

## Built for real hangouts, not just silent matches

Online rooms aren't only about moves on a board. You can:

- **Chat** with everyone in the room
- **Turn on video and audio** when you want face-to-face game night
- **Customize the lobby** — player count, timers, and room privacy where the game allows it
- **Rejoin and recover** if someone drops — sessions are designed to handle real-world connectivity

It's meant to feel like sitting around a table, not staring at a cold scoreboard.
